Color Usage Guide
Recommended Colors:
  • Red: Critical, Important, Urgent
  • Orange: Warning, Caution, Review
  • Yellow: Pending, In Progress
  • Green: Success, Completed, Approved
Additional Options:
  • Cyan: Information, General
  • Blue: Reference, Documentation
  • Purple: Special, Custom
  • Gray: Archive, Inactive
